AirNest protects urban birds by turning waste into bird-friendly features.

AirNest is an innovative start-up dedicated to urban ecological protection and sustainable development.

We aim to protect urban bird populations by recycling discarded outdoor appliances into bird-friendly features, combining environmental conservation, technology, and community engagement to create a harmonious urban ecosystem.

The project is currently in the preliminary round of the
2025 Forbes China GenZ Entrepreneurship Program Competition

Main Services

We provide a complete end-to-end ecological service.

Inspection of AC Outdoor Units

We collaborate with a professional maintenance team to deliver regular, professional inspections of outdoor AC units for residents, identifying units suitable for recycling and ensuring optimal performance.

Replacement and Installation Services

Outdated or damaged AC units are replaced with eco-friendly, energy-efficient models. We manage the entire process, including assessment, removal, and installation, ensuring minimal disruption.

After-Sales Service (The Nest)

As a unique addition, we repurpose parts of the recycled units to create and install bespoke "AirNests." These are aesthetically designed, weather-resistant birdhouses that provide safe nesting for urban birds.

How We Start: Research & Concept

Our solution is built on a deep understanding of the ecological landscape and stakeholder needs.

Design Insight

Our research identified a massive, untapped resource: 43% of AC units are on property, with 32% being easily accessible. We found that residents and property managers are highly motivated to recycle but lack a clear, efficient process.

The Stakeholders

Our ecosystem connects everyone: Users (residents) gain a simple service, Partners (maintenance teams) get more business, Government meets sustainability goals, and End-Users (the birds) get new homes.

The Solution: A Digital Platform

To manage this ecosystem, we designed a two-part mobile application.

Mobile Application Service

We designed two versions of the UI to serve both sides of our marketplace:

  • User Version: A streamlined booking interface, community features, and a bird-watching/protection-sharing platform to encourage engagement.
  • Service Provider Version: A robust dashboard for maintenance teams to manage service requests, optimize routes, and manage their work schedules.

Market Opportunity

Our platform solves key problems for both residents and property managers, replacing a disorganized, high-friction process with a streamlined, transparent, and profitable system.
